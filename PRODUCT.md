# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local guests, visitors to Sevilla, and wine-and-tapas seekers who want a welcoming place with personality rather than a generic restaurant landing page. They may arrive on mobile while deciding where to go next, so the site must make the mood, offer, language switch, and location immediately clear.

## Product Purpose

Tabanco Olé Tú presents a family-run tabanco with wine, tapas, and a warm atmosphere. Success means visitors understand the place quickly, feel the family character, can switch between Spanish and English, and can open the location map without friction.

## Brand Personality

Warm, familiar, and refined. The brand should feel like a clean black-and-white wine label placed on cream paper with botanical wine-bar illustration: elegant, minimal, friendly, and not overdesigned.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic restaurant templates, beige rustic clichés, noisy food-delivery layouts, heavy stock-photo collages, purple-blue gradients, glassmorphism, oversized rounded cards, and repeated AI-style section labels.

## Design Principles

- Let the logo lead; the supplied title artwork is the strongest hero asset.
- Use deep green, sage, cream, pale gold, and lavender grape accents as the visual world.
- Keep the first screen full-width and brand-led: centered title artwork, promise, reservation path, and location cue.
- Make Spanish the default voice and English a first-class alternate.
- Prefer full-bleed bands and controlled botanical decoration over card-heavy layouts or unrelated imagery.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, 44px touch targets, responsive layouts from mobile to desktop, reduced-motion support, semantic landmarks, and language-aware page updates for Spanish and English.
